FAQ

why i haven't received my design?
If you haven't received your design, it's because you entered the wrong email address. Before you send me a message, please double-check all the fields you entered. If you have not received your design within 1-7 days, please contact me via this email: mxsimgraphics124@gmail.com
How do I buy one of the designs?
First, order the design you want me to make. After I make your design, I send you a proof and if everything is satisfactory, then I send you an invoice and when I receive the money, I send you a file with the design.
Can I Order Design that is not on your design catalog?
Yes, you can! Choose what you want to order: Honda Yamaha KTM Husqvarna GasGas Kawasaki Gear
Do you make design in real life too?
No, I make design only for games which is mx simulator and mx bikes and maybe in the future i will make design for other mx games too.
How frequently do you release new designs and gear for the games?
I try to release designs once every 1 or 2 weeks.
Do you offer seasonal discounts on your dirt bike and gear designs?
Yes, we will offer seasonal discounts such as spring and summer discounts.
If I buy a design for a team, do I pay for each rider's design or is it one price for everything together
You pay for the design of each rider. Example (3 team riders * 15$ = 45$) and the same is the case with gear designs
How can I stay updated on new releases for your dirt bike and gear designs?
Follow us on Instagram and join our discord server to be informed about new design releases.
